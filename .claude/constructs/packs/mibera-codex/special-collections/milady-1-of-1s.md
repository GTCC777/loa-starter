# Milady 1-of-1s

**Type:** Tribute, 1/1

Five custom Miberas created as direct tributes to specific [Milady Maker](https://miladymaker.net/) NFTs and their stories. Each is a hand-crafted 1-of-1 within the 10,000 generative collection — all share the [Milady](../core-lore/archetypes.md#milady) archetype and [Rank F](../traits/overlays/ranking/f.md).

Unlike the [Grails](../grails/README.md), which are fully hand-drawn 1/1 art pieces, Milady 1-of-1s are generative Miberas with unique traits that appear nowhere else in the collection. Every trait exclusive to a Milady 1-of-1 exists on exactly one token out of 10,000.

## Shared Characteristics

All five Milady 1-of-1s share:

- **Archetype:** [Milady](../core-lore/archetypes.md#milady) (Capricorn/Aquarius/Pisces)
- **Swag Rank:** [F](../traits/overlays/ranking/f.md) — the "Special" tier, reserved for tokens that operate outside the normal scoring system
- **Time Period:** Modern
- **Low Swag Scores:** Ranging from 2–7, reflecting the F-rank's independence from the multiplicative [swag scoring formula](../swag-scoring/README.md)

## How to Identify a Milady 1-of-1

If a Mibera has:
1. **Milady archetype** + **Rank F**
2. One or more traits that appear on **no other token** in the collection
3. A trait name that references a specific Milady token ID (e.g., "Milady 5930 Crescent And Dots")

...it is one of the five Milady 1-of-1 tributes documented here.
