# Team History

> **Note:** This document was aggregated from the Mibera work server (Discord) being scraped. The information here reflects what was captured at the time of scraping and is subject to change. Names, roles, and contributions may be incomplete or outdated.

*The people who built Mibera — from a Discord server to 10,000 time-travelling bears.*

---

## Overview

Mibera was built by a small, geographically distributed team operating primarily through Discord and Google Drive over 18+ months. The project originated from the intersection of the Milady NFT community and the Berachain ecosystem, funded through THJ (The Honey Jar).

## Core Team

### Jani (deepname99)

**Role:** Founder, philosophical architect, creative director, project lead

Jani is the mind behind Mibera's philosophical framework. He wrote the founding mythology, designed the economic model, managed IP and finances, and served as the acknowledged "pirate captain" with final decision-making authority on all key matters.

**Key contributions:**
- Authored the entire lore system — the Refusal, ravepill vs. clearpill, the Kaironic/Chronic time dichotomy
- Designed the Mibera Economy Model and Tributaries system for derivative projects
- Created the astrology-tribe integration ("so that we get some additional memetic surface area for free")
- Color-coded the trait spreadsheet by archetype to ensure tribe balance
- Managed the relationship with Remilia/Charlotte Fang, submitting lore to Remilia Quarterly
- Called the trait pause that prevented scope creep
- Defined THJ ownership of all IP and creative output

**Influences:** CCRU (Cybernetic Culture Research Unit), Hakim Bey's Temporary Autonomous Zones, Plato's Cave, rave culture history. Key insight: Mibera is not a Milady clone but its philosophical shadow.

> *"Mibera is Daria but on drugs. Mibera is Lisa Simpson but on drugs."*

> *"MIBERA IS A FULLY AUTONOMOUS UNGOVERNABLE TREASURY RAVE. MIBERA REFUSES MILADY."*

**Active:** August 2023 — ongoing

### Gumi (gumibera_)

**Role:** Lead artist, trait designer, art director

Gumi painted every single trait in the 1,337+ collection on iPad using Procreate. She established the impressionist, no-outline, "smeary" style that defines Mibera's visual identity and managed the complex multi-layer rendering system.

**Key contributions:**
- Created the entire PFP art system — all body types, eyes, hair, mouths, eyebrows, shirts, hats, masks, items, backgrounds, face accessories
- Made the critical pivot from full-body to portrait format
- Managed tributary artist relationships
- Wore out an Apple Pencil nib rendering hairstyles

**Style:** Blocks of color over linework. Visible brushstrokes. Desaturated bodies so saturated traits pop.

> *"Genuinely think that this is exactly what I needed to get this style change grokking right in my damn head. Mibera has fire in her eyes, she's not just a laid back docile bear rendered in a cutesy scuffed way."*

**Active:** July 2023 — ongoing

## How the Team Formed

### Phase 1: The Founding Circle (July 2023)

The project started on July 27, 2023 when **tez33** set up the Discord server and pinged the initial crew: **balensci**, **lilbobross**, **glooming** (Kata), and **gumibera_**. These were, as Jani later described them, "the resident Milady x Bera aficionados." They thought Milady and Berachain had something in common but couldn't yet articulate what.

### Phase 2: The Manifesto (August 2023)

On August 14, 2023, Jani delivered his "MIBERA IS THE REFUSAL" session. Gumi immediately understood the art direction, Kata declared Jani had given them purpose, and lilbobross said Jani was "on par with Charlotte philosophical word vomit... but I love it."

### Phase 3: Division of Labor (September - October 2023)

The team naturally separated into domains:
- **Jani** — Philosophy, lore, scoring, strategic direction
- **Gumi** — All trait art production
- **Tez** — Visual direction, mood boards, pattern sourcing, server admin
- **Gods** — Marketing graphics, animations, memes
- **0xuptake** — Music curation and audio direction
- **Kata + Lilbobross** — Cultural validation and design feedback

### Phase 4: Expansion (January - March 2024)

The project opened up for artist onboarding and marketing. **Fedor** was brought on specifically for marketing. The tributary system was designed to allow external artists to create derivative works under defined IP terms.

### Phase 5: The Long Wait (April 2024 - February 2025)

Berachain mainnet delays stretched the timeline from an April 2024 target to early 2025. The team maintained cohesion through this period, continuing to refine traits and coordinate tributary artists.

## Decision-Making

The project operated with **Jani as acknowledged leader** — explicitly established on March 1, 2024 when he laid out the formal IP and governance structure. But the creative process was genuinely collaborative:

- **Philosophy:** Jani would post extended riffs; the team challenged, refined, and validated
- **Visual direction:** Tez, Gumi, and the team iterated in real-time through Discord and voice calls
- **Art execution:** Gumi had significant autonomy, with Jani and Tez providing philosophical and aesthetic guardrails
- **Trait balance:** Collaborative spreadsheet work with Jani color-coding by tribe, Gumi offering to fill gaps, and the team collectively agreeing that perfect balance was less important than authentic imbalance
