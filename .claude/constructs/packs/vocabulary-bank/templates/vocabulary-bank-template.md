## The Core Rule

Every word exists at the intersection of two registers:

| Register | Purpose | When it fires |
|----------|---------|---------------|
| **Layer 1: Operational** | Clarity, chain-standard terms | Confirmations, errors, transaction states, button labels |
| **Layer 2: World** | Immersion, product identity, discovery | Entrance, idle states, descriptions, tooltips, exploration |

**The boundary rule**: Layer 2 vocabulary may appear in discovery moments. Layer 1 vocabulary MUST appear in confirmation moments. When in doubt, use Layer 1.
