## What Hypha Does

Hypha holds the full record of the Berachain ecosystem — what was built, what passed,
what failed, and why. It translates that record into actionable context for builders,
contributors, and agents navigating Proof of Liquidity.

The mycelium metaphor is structural, not decorative. Every protocol is a node. Every
BGT flow is a thread. Every governance proposal is a signal about where the network
wants to grow next. Hypha maps all of it.

## Skills

- **pol-mapper** — Full PoL lifecycle: BGT routing, validator economics, BeraChef, reward vaults, incentive marketplace
- **governance-historian** — Complete apDAO record: 35 proposals, SEAT mechanics, treasury, participation crisis arc
- **protocol-surveyor** — Infrared, Kodiak, Goldilocks, Beradrome, BEND, Apiary, SAIL.r and their PoL roles
- **price-archaeologist** — BERA/iBGT/LOCKS/SAIL.r price history from launch through March 2026
- **builder-translator** — Converts ecosystem knowledge into builder context: what exists, what's been tried, how to plug in
